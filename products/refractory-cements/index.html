CATEGORY · REFRACTORY CEMENTS

All types of Refractory Cement & Castables

Every bag of our refractory cement is the result of an eight-stage manufacturing discipline — from sourcing high-grade raw bauxite and standard quality limestone, through individual powdering, thorough mixing, nodulization, high-temperature calcination and ball-mill crushing, finishing with rigorous sample testing and high-quality packaging.
