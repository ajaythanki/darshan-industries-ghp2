CATEGORY · CALCINED BAUXITE

High Alumina Calcined Bauxite

Raw bauxite is procured and lab-tested before calcination into lumps. Material is then crushed and graded into defined sizes, with fines produced in controlled mesh ranges for specialist applications.

Specifications & forms

Calcined Bauxite — Lumps

Directly sold lumps of calcined bauxite for refractory and metallurgical applications.

Calcined Bauxite — Grains

Crushed and graded calcined bauxite, available in defined size fractions.

  • 0 – 1 mm
  • 1 – 3 mm
  • 3 – 5 mm

Calcined Bauxite — Fines

Micron-fine calcined bauxite for refractory formulations and specialist uses.

  • 80 mesh
  • 100 mesh
  • 150 mesh
  • 180 mesh
