ABOUT US

Building refractory excellence since 2008

Darshan Refractories stands as a premier manufacturer of refractory materials, combining decades of expertise with cutting-edge technology to deliver solutions that withstand the most extreme industrial conditions.

OUR STORY

From humble beginnings to industry leadership

Established in 2008 by visionary entrepreneurs in Porbandar, Gujarat, Darshan Refractories began as a small-scale refractory material supplier with a commitment to quality that would become our hallmark. Over the years, we've grown into a trusted name serving major industrial players across India and beyond.

Our journey has been defined by continuous innovation, investment in technology, and an unwavering focus on customer satisfaction. Today, we operate from a state-of-the-art facility in Dharampur GIDC, Porbandar, manufacturing products that meet and exceed international quality standards.
