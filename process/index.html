03 — MANUFACTURING

From raw earth to refractory-grade material.

Our two core processes are executed with strict lab oversight at every stage. Each step is documented, verified, and quality-assured to ensure premium-grade materials.

PROCESS 01 · REFRACTORY CEMENT

How we make Refractory Cement

  1. 01

    Sourcing

    High-grade raw bauxite and standard quality limestone are selected from trusted mines.

    Step 1
  2. 02

    Processing

    Both raw materials are individually processed into fine powder.

    Step 2
  3. 03

    Mixing

    Powdered bauxite and limestone are thoroughly mixed in controlled ratios.

    Step 3
  4. 04

    Nodulization

    The homogenous mixture is formed into uniform nodules.

    Step 4
  5. 05

    Calcination

    Nodules are fired at high temperature to develop refractory properties.

    Step 5
  6. 06

    Crushing

    Calcined material is crushed via ball milling to target fineness.

    Step 6
  7. 07

    Quality Control

    Every batch is sample-tested in our in-house lab.

    Step 7
  8. 08

    Packaging

    Standard, high-quality packaging ready for dispatch.

    Step 8
PROCESS 02 · CALCINED BAUXITE

Calcined Bauxite Production

  1. 01

    Sourcing & Testing

    High-grade raw bauxite is procured and material properties are lab-tested.

    Step 1
  2. 02

    Calcination

    Material is calcined into lumps — also sold directly as lumps.

    Step 2
  3. 03

    Grinding & Grading

    Calcined material is crushed into defined grain sizes: 0–1 mm, 1–3 mm and 3–5 mm.

    Step 3
  4. 04

    Fines Processing

    Fines are produced in a controlled 80 to 180 mesh range.

    Step 4
