01 — WHO WE ARE

A refractory manufacturer built on discipline.

Established in 2008, Darshan Refractories is a trusted manufacturer of all types of refractory cements 50%, castables and high alumina calcined bauxite. From our facility in Porbandar, Gujarat, we supply industries that demand uncompromising heat-resistance and material integrity.
